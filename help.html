Creating New Account:

To Create New Account, click on Sign Up and fill up required entries marked with "*"
or all entries including (optional), then click Register button to register.
All entries marked with "*" must be filled in otherwise the registration will not be granted.

Login to Account:

To Login to account you must already have created account prior Login, in order to Login
click on Login and enter your username or email that you have used during the registration, enter
password and click Sign In button.

Update Account Details:

Updating account details requires you to be signed in to your account, to update account
details go to Settings page by clicking on Settings, click on to Update My Account
Details, then change any information e.g. email address, phone number etc... and
once details have been entered to change outdated information, then click
"Update Account Details" button.

Change Password:

In order to change the password you can either enter username or email address
that you have used during the registration, old password must be entered for security
to confirm change the password, then enter new password and repeat the same new
password to ensure that new password has been entered correctly without
mistyping the characters of new password that could prevent you from
signing in due to wrong password. Once entered username or email, old password,
new password and re-entered new password inside change password form, click the
button "Change Password" and your account password will be changed.

View My Profile Details:

To view your registered profile details you must login to your account, once you
have signed in to your account then click on "Settings" then click on "My Account Details"
and you should be able to see your registered account data. If you would like to make changes
to your account details such as update account details or change password, you can click on
"Update Account Details" or "Update Account Password" which are both located inside the
account details page.

Delete or Deactivate Account:

Before deleting or deactivating your existing account you must have logged
into your account, once logged in then click on "Settings" located on top of the
page, then select "Delete/Deactivate Account", once you access delete account page you need
to enter your username or email address, then enter your password and
repeat password and finally click on "Delete My Account" to delete your account
permanently or click "Deactivate My Account" button to only deactivate your account
which will not delete your account nor your uploads, but hides your profile only.
